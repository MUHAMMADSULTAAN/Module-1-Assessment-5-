  • Notable Foes: Black Widow has faced numerous adversaries, including Ivan Vanko, Batroc the Leaper, and various Hydra agents. She has also battled fellow heroes during the "Civil War" storyline.
  • Team Player: A prominent member of the Avengers, Black Widow often collaborates with other heroes to combat larger threats, showcasing her dedication and loyalty to her teammates.
